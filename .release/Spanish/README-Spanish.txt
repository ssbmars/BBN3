
	BBN3 Spanish


----------------
  How to Use
-----------------

Use the included flips.exe program to patch BBN3_Spanish.bps over a clean BN3 Blue ROM.
It must be the Blue version of BN3 (English), and cannot be combined with any other patches.


Read the changelog at https://n1gp.dev/BBN3/changelog

Record your matches and post videos. Have fun!
